About Us

We are the UN Refugee Agency’s national partner in Australia, raising awareness and funds to support UNHCR’s global emergency response to humanitarian crises.
Together we will provide life-changing support for the millions of refugees.
Australia for UNHCR
fundraises in many ways, with regular donations from everyday Australians underpinning much of its work. Other fundraising includes supporting community events, corporate partnerships and foundation work. We also deliver education and advocacy initiatives to raise
public awareness
of the plight of displaced people around the world and UNHCR’s work to protect and care for them. We were established in 2000 and has grown to be one of UNHCR’s
top ten
private sector contributors globally, ahead of many governments and institutions. In 2018, we raised $32.5 million to support UNHCR’s urgent humanitarian responses across the world.
