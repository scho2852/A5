Be The Change

UNHCR, the UN Refugee Agency, is a global organization dedicated to saving lives, protecting rights and building a better future for refugees, forcibly displaced communities and stateless people.
"Australians Empowering Refugees To Build A Better Future"
WHO ARE WE?
We are the UN Refugee Agency’s national partner in Australia, raising awareness and funds to support UNHCR’s global emergency response to humanitarian crises.
OUR IMPACT
We deliver education and advocacy initiatives to raise public awareness of the plight of displaced people around the world and UNHCR’s work to protect and care for them. We were established in 2000 and has grown to be one of UNHCR’s top ten private sector contributors globally, ahead of many governments and institutions.
