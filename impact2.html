Our Impact

Australia for UNHCR was established in 2000 and has grown to be one of UNHCR’s top ten private sector contributors globally, ahead of many governments and institutions. In 2018, Australia for UNHCR raised $32.5 million to support UNHCR’s urgent humanitarian responses across the world.
Highlights from 2019 Annual Report
The majority of funds raised by Australia for UNHCR are directed to UNHCR’s
emergency operations
providing the ready funds and resources to respond
quickly and effectively
in situations of crisis and disaster. UNHCR works in 134 countries, supporting refugees and displaced people at every stage of their journey – at the height of emergency, in established camps and increasingly in urban areas of host communities, and where possible on their eventual return or resettlement in another country.
